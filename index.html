About

Hi, I'm Nimrod, a tech enthusiast with a passion for web development. My journey started in 2019 when I dove into project management, eventually leading me to a startup where I played a key role in creating an online marketplace for local trades and construction services.

Since then, I've evolved from project management to becoming a product owner and a technical customer success manager in cybersecurity. Fast forward to 2022, and I made a dynamic shift into the world of front-end web development. This move allowed me to fuse my project management expertise with a desire to learn and build new technology, making me a versatile asset for any team.

Committed to delivering outstanding web experiences, I'm eager to connect with professionals and explore opportunities in the dynamic tech landscape. Let's tackle challenges together, transforming them into opportunities for growth and innovation. Excited to create something amazing together!

Score Counter

This app is a simple scoreboard for tracking points in a two-team competition. Users can easily increase the score for each team by clicking buttons representing different point values. Additionally, there are reset buttons available to clear the scores for both teams, providing a straightforward way to manage and display scores during games or events.
